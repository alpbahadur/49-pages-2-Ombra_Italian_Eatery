The Legacy of Chef Alfredo Esposito

Born in Abruzzo, Italy, Chef Alfredo Esposito carries forward a 130-year family culinary tradition. His journey from the hills of Italy to the vibrant streets of San Juan has been marked by Michelin recognition and an unwavering commitment to authentic Italian cuisine.

At Ombra, Chef Esposito weaves together traditional recipes passed down through generations with innovative Mediterranean techniques, creating an experience that honors the past while embracing the present.

"In every dish, I tell the story of my famiglia, my terra, and my passione."
